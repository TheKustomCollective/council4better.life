## Living Legacy Launches Innovative End-of-Life Concierge Services to Bring Peace of Mind to Families Worldwide

*Revolutionary platform combines compassionate human support with secure technology to transform end-of-life planning*

---

**[Las Vegas, Nevada] – [12/01/2025]** – Living Legacy, a Council for Better Life initiative, today announced the launch of its comprehensive end-of-life concierge services designed to provide families with peace of mind during life's most challenging transitions. The innovative platform combines personalized human support with cutting-edge secure technology to make end-of-life planning accessible, dignified, and stress-free.

### Addressing a Critical Need

More than 70% of Americans don't have documented end-of-life plans, leaving families to make difficult decisions during times of grief. Living Legacy addresses this gap by offering:

- **24/7 Concierge Support**: Personal coordinators available around the clock
- **LegacyShield® Digital Vault**: Bank-level encrypted storage for essential documents
- **Sequoia Funeral Concierge Plan**: Comprehensive funeral and memorial coordination
- **Family Communication Tools**: Facilitate important conversations with loved ones

### Key Features

**For Individuals Planning Ahead:**
- Free initial consultations with certified planners
- Secure document storage and easy family sharing
- Customizable service packages from basic planning to comprehensive concierge support
- Educational resources and community support

**For Families Facing Loss:**
- Immediate 24/7 emergency response
- Full-service funeral and memorial coordination
- Legal and financial paperwork guidance
- Ongoing grief resources and support

### Technology Meets Compassion

Living Legacy's platform leverages modern technology while maintaining the essential human touch:
- Mobile-accessible secure portal
- Automated reminders and notifications
- Integration with healthcare providers and legal professionals
- Multilingual support for diverse communities

Living Legacy offers flexible pricing options including:
- Free basic planning tools
- Affordable annual subscriptions starting at $99
- Lifetime memberships for one-time payment
- Insurance coordination and payment plan options
